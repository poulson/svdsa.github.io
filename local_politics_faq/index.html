  • What is the easiest way to look up my local representatives?
    California provides a convenient tool: Find Your California Representative. After entering in your address, it should provide links to your representatives for the State Assembly and State Senate.

    Your representatives for the US Senate are Kamala D. Harris and Dianne Feinstein.
  • What is the best way to keep track of recent/active bills in the California legislature?
    The best place to start is the official resource: leginfo.legislature.ca.gov .

  • What about our county-level officials?
    Santa Clara County is governed by a five-member Board of Supervisors responsible for enacting and repealing local ordinances, approving the county budget, overseeing county departments, approving and reviewing public programs, and approving land use and zoning plans. Each member represents one of the five districts in Santa Clara County.

    Supervisors are elected every four years and can serve a maximum of three terms. The Board meets on alternate Tuesdays. Other county-level officials include the County Assessor, District Attorney, County Sheriff, and County Executive. Information on these officials, including contact information, can be found on the Santa Clara County website.
